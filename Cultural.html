Endurance Reloaded

Description :
* This is a race based event in which the entries will be in pairs.
* At the end of every round a team will be eliminated on performance basis.
* The race will be done in a three phase protocol.
* The team winning the grand finale will be announced as the winning team.
Key to Success(PRELIMINARY ROUND) :
In this round any numbers of participants are entertained but after this round only ten teams (team of 2) will proceed further for the first halve of the event.
>> Fastest Will Survive
FIRST HALVE
In this halve the 10 qualified teams would be taking part in a series of events including physical challenges etc. and at the end of each round a team will get eliminated.
>> This halve of the event will be completed on the first day of the fest.
SECOND HALVE
In this halve the above left 5 teams will continue with the race to reach the grand finale. This part will again constitute of various new activities, hurdles and tasks. By the end of this halve only two teams will survive for the grand finale.
Grand Finale
The grand finale will be the most difficult and a long race in which the teams will have to go back to all the places at which they had performed their tasks. What they have to do can’t be revealed since the task will be an on spot task.
