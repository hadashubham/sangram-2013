SANGRAM 2013


SANGRAM 2013 is the annual techno-management fest organized by the students of LAXMI DEVI INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY.

This is a three day long extravaganza that comprises of events like technical events, robotics events, choreography, star night and many other competitions which will bring out the hidden talents of college students.

Loud music, tapping feet, excitement and an enthusiastic crowd will be the general scene at SANGRAM.

It will have competitions modeled to test the technical acumen, programming skills, flair for business management, dancing and other cultural abilities of the participants. It is a festival that commences weeks before the actual dates, in the form of online contests and various other social initiatives.

SANGRAM 2013 is the official technical, cultural and youth fest to be hosted by Laxmi Devi College of Engineering and Technology.
